Locations


As a Chicago-based project, most of the nodes that currently exist are located there. As of January 2020 there were roughly 130 nodes installed in the city.

The team continues to seek research and development funding to expand, evolve and improve the system with additional node locations and with new technologies in the coming years.

The City of Chicago website has an interactive, updated map showing the Array of Things Locations. Additionally, a static map of locations can be found below.
