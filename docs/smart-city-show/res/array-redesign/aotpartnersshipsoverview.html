AoT PARTNERSHIPS


Connecting Beyond Chicago

“It’s very exciting to see our Chicago-born seedling bloom into a global collaboration.”
--Charlie Catlett, founding director of the Urban Center for Computation and Data


The Array of Things team encourages partner deployments of the sensor network to cities and municipalities both in the US and internationally. More than 100 cities across the world interested in partnering with us have reached out since AoT was founded in 2015!

We'd love to count you among them.

AoT has also developed a Lane of Things curriculum for Chicago's Lane Technical Prep High School. The School of Things has also been developed for middle and high school computer science teachers in Chicago. Though not currently available at other schools or cities, let us know if this is something you'd like to explore!
